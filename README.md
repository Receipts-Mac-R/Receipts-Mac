## Overview

Receipts Professional Suite is a complete expense documentation platform that serves freelancers, businesses, and accountants with the export flexibility, reporting depth, and multi-currency support needed for professional expense management and tax preparation. It extends receipt scanning into comprehensive expense reporting with features that produce the documentation that accountants and tax authorities require.

Export to accounting software produces categorized expense data in formats compatible with QuickBooks, Xero, DATEV, and other accounting platforms, enabling seamless transfer of expense data from receipt capture to financial records. CSV and PDF export options handle custom export requirements, and the export templates can be configured to match specific accounting system import formats. Tax-category-organized exports simplify tax preparation by grouping deductible expenses by tax line.

Multi-currency handling processes receipts in any currency with automatic exchange rate lookup for the transaction date, enabling expense tracking for international travel and cross-border business without manual currency conversion. The application maintains spending records in both the original transaction currency and the home currency, providing accurate expense reporting regardless of where purchases were made.

Report generation creates expense summaries, category breakdowns, monthly comparisons, and custom reports that provide the financial overview needed for budget review, client billing, and tax documentation. Reports include receipt images alongside extracted data, providing complete documentation that satisfies audit requirements. Report templates save frequently used configurations for recurring reporting needs.

Receipts Professional Suite is the expense documentation platform for professionals who need comprehensive receipt management with the export, reporting, and multi-currency capability that professional financial management and tax compliance demand. Its automation of the capture-to-report pipeline makes consistent expense documentation achievable even for users who have historically avoided receipt tracking.

## Performance & Integration

Receipts is engineered around macOS technologies that handle performance-critical work efficiently. Tasks run in the background without slowing down the rest of your system — you can keep working while Receipts processes operations quietly behind the scenes.

The app integrates deeply with the macOS ecosystem out of the box:

- Launch workflows directly through **Spotlight**
- Preview files instantly with **Quick Look**
- Trigger actions from any app via the **Services menu**
- Fully compatible with **Shortcuts** for automated workflows
